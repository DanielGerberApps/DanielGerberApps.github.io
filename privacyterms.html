1. Privacy Policy

The Shabbat Alarm Clock app (the "App") is provided by Daniel Gerber ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers, from you or your device. The App operates fully offline regarding user data, meaning your alarm settings, customized ringtones, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Local Data Storage

The App stores data locally on your device to function correctly, which **includes but is not limited to** the following:

  • Alarm schedules and settings (e.g., time, enabled/disabled status).
  • Ringtone selection and recordings you make within the App.
  • App configuration preferences (e.g., date formats).

This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Permissions and Device Access

The App may request permission to access the following device features, which are necessary for the App's primary functions:

  • **Microphone:** Used only for recording custom ringtones if you choose to use that feature. The recording stays on your device.
  • **Notifications/Alarms:** Necessary to trigger the alarms at the scheduled times, even when the App is closed.
  • **Advertising Identifier:** This identifier is accessed by our third-party advertising partners (Google AdMob, Unity) for the purposes of displaying personalized ads, capping ad frequency, and analytics.
